Pranav Mamidanna

PhD Candidate

Aalborg University

I am interested in building assistive and adaptive technology! To this end, I am currently developing closed-loop interfaces for EMG-driven hand prostheses by combining algorithms that faithfully decode user intention with intuitive tactile feedback.

I am a PhD candidate in the lab of Strahinja Dosen at the Health Science and Technology Department in Aalborg University, Denmark. I was previously a research assistant in the lab of Matthias Bethge, where I worked on neural network models of the proprioceptive system with Alexander Mathis.
